About US

We are NASA — the National Aeronautics and Space Administration — the United States' space agency,

leading the way in space exploration, scientific discovery, and aeronautics research.

Since our founding in 1958, we've been on a mission to explore space, study our home planet, and unlock

the secrets of the universe. From landing the first humans on the Moon to launching powerful telescopes

that look deep into the cosmos, our work continues to push the boundaries of what's possible.

But NASA is more than just missions and milestones. We're made up of engineers, scientists, astronauts,

educators, and visionaries — all working together to:

  • Expand human presence in space
  • Conduct cutting-edge research and innovation
  • Inspire the world through bold achievements
  • Collaborate with global partners to benefit all of humanity
