Fun Facts About Earth

  1. Earth is the only planet with life (that we know of).

    2. It’s the ultimate VIP of the solar system — full of plants, animals, and… memes.

  2. 70% of Earth’s surface is covered in water.

    3. So technically, it should be called “Ocean,” right?

  3. Earth isn’t a perfect sphere.

    4. Because of its spin, Earth is slightly squished at the poles — it's more like a chubby ball.

  4. You’re spinning at over 1,000 mph right now.

    5. Even if you’re just lying in bed. Earth rotates that fast at the equator!

  5. Earth’s atmosphere protects us.

    6. It blocks harmful space radiation, burns up meteors, and lets us breathe — a true superhero layer!

  6. Clouds can weigh over a million pounds.

    7. Luckily, they float because the water is spread out — science magic in the sky!

  7. Earth is constantly recycling itself.

    8. Through plate tectonics, mountains rise, oceans shift, and continents move like giant slow dancers.

  8. You always see the same side of the Moon from Earth.

    9. Because of synchronous rotation — the Moon’s just shy like that.

  9. Earth is loud… in space terms.

    10. We send out radio waves, signals, and space probes — basically shouting "HELLO!" into the galaxy.
