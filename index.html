Our Mission

At NASA, our mission is to explore the unknown for the benefit of humanity.

We are driven by curiosity and powered by science, innovation, and teamwork.

From launching satellites that study Earth’s climate to sending spacecraft beyond our solar system,

every mission is a step forward in our journey to understand the universe and our place within it.

NASA is not just about rockets and astronauts. It’s about discovery, imagination, and building a future where

anything is possible. We’re here to go farther, reach higher, and achieve more—together.
